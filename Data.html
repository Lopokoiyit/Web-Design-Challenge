Comparisons: Latitude vs. X

The graphs show an indepth analysis. As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interesting, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends ot be warmer this time of year than the nothern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
